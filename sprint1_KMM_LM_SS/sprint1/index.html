The Echo Waves


The Echo Waves are a band from the UK. We are a 4 piece band that specialises in rock music. We have been playing together for over 10 years and have previously released 3 albums. We have toured all over Europe and the UK and have a the absolute greatest fans.

Our 4th album, The Final Break, was just released. We are about to kick off our very first world tour to rock out with all of you. We can't wait to be back on the road!
